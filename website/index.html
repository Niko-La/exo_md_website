Why is this worth fighting for?

Why do we think this is likely?

The barriers to running frontier AI models are increasing. Models are getting bigger and with reasoning models like o1, more compute is being used at inference time.

What are we going to do about it?

The mission of exo is to democratise access to AI.

The way we will do that is by building open frontier models that can run anywhere.

Who are we?

We are cypherpunks: advocates for privacy, freedom, and decentralization.

We are effective accelerationists: we believe that technology is a force for good that should be unrestricted (it's the applications of technology that should be restricted).

We are techno-optimists: we believe that societies, like sharks, grow or die, and everything good is downstream of growth.
