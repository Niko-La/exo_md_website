Without immediate and decisive action, the benefits of AI will be concentrated in the hands of a few.

The mission of Exo Labs is to subvert this tragedy. To build a future where AI is owned by everyone, not just a few.

Exo Labs builds software that restores the balance of power to individuals using cryptography, anonymous technologies, digital signatures, and with electronic money. The first step in this mission is an open-source library that enables anyone to run large AI models on consumer devices.
